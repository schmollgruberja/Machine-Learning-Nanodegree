HASYv2 dataset symbol recognition¶

In this project we will take a look at the fields of hand written symbol recognition. Handwriting recognition (HWR) belongs to the domain of image processing. Let's take a closer look to our dataset. We used the HASYv2 dataset to train Machine Learning algorithms to recognize 369 mathematical symbols.

In this jupyter notebook we will use the simple test- split model evaluation approach. We decided this regarding time- saving purposes.

To see the 10-fold validation & evaluation approach look at README.md.
